Nutmeg and Cayeene
Spiced Chicken
with a
Hummus, Pear,
and Bell Pepper Slaw

Ingredients

Hummus, Pear, and Bell Pepper Slaw

  • 4 tbsp Hummus
  • 1 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 1/2 Lemon
  • 2 tsp Fig Spread
  • 2 tsp Apricot Jam

  • 1 Package of Organic Brocolli Slaw
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Orange Bell Pepper
  • 2 Pears

Nutmeg and Cayeene Spiced Chicken

  • 4 Organic Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breasts
  • For each chicken breast...
    • 1 tsp Cayeene Pepper
    • 1 tsp Nutmeg
    • 1 Lemon Slice
    • Pinch of salt
    • Pinch of lemon zest

Method

Line a baking sheet with parchment paper, and place one chicken breast on it

Add the cayeene pepper, nutmeg, and salt

Add the lemon zest

Top the chicken breast with a lemon slice

Wrap the chicken "en papillote"

Repeat the process with the remaining three chicken breasts. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 - 30 minutes

Add the hummus, apple cider vinegar, lemon, fig spread, and apricot jam into a measuring cup

Stir all of the ingredients together

Separate the orange bell pepper into sections

Chop the bell pepper into approximately 1 inch pieces. Repeat with the red bell pepper

Separate the pear into sections

Chop the pear into approximately 1 inch pieces. Repeat with the other pear.

Combine the dressing, red and orange bell peppers, and pears

Take the chicken breasts out of the oven.

Unwrap each chicken breast

This is what the chicken breasts should look like when they are finished cooking

Place a chicken breast and a portion of the slaw into a container

All done!
